Teen Online Safety Guide

Welcome to the Teen Online Safety Guide! Here, you'll find valuable tips to stay safe while exploring the digital world.

Password Security

Choose strong, unique passwords for each online account. Use a combination of uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols.

Social Media Awareness

Be mindful of what you share on social media. Adjust privacy settings and think twice before posting personal information or photos.

Cyberbullying Prevention

Stand against cyberbullying. Report any inappropriate behavior online and support your friends who might be victims.

Mobile App Safety

Review and understand the privacy settings of mobile apps. Only download apps from trusted sources, and be cautious with in-app purchases.
