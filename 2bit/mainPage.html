What is Sex Education?

Sex education is a broad term that encompasses the teaching and learning of
various aspects of human sexuality, reproductive health, and relationships.
Sex education typically covers anatomy, reproductive systems, contraception,
sexually transmitted infections (STIs), consent, healthy relationships, and
other related subjects. Comprehensive sex education is considered important
for promoting informed decision-making, reducing the risk of unplanned
pregnancies and STIs, and fostering healthy relationships.

Mental Health

Mental health refers to a person's emotional, psychological, and social well-being.
It affects how individuals think, feel, and act, influencing their ability to handle stress,
relate to others, and make choices. Mental health is important at every stage of life,
from childhood and adolescence through adulthood.This involves understanding and managing
one's emotions effectively,as well as being able to cope with life's challenges.
This includes cognitive functions such as problem-solving, decision-making, and memory.
It also involves having a positive outlook and a sense of purpose.

Why is Mental Health Important?

It's important to clarify that mental health itself is not inherently dangerous.
Mental health refers to a person's emotional, psychological, and social well-being,
and maintaining good mental health is crucial for overall well-being. Mental
health challenges or disorders, however, can be significant and pose risks to an individual's health
and functioning
