Friendship Guide

Explore insights and advice on building and nurturing meaningful friendships that contribute to your well-being.

Being a Good Friend

Practice empathy, active listening, and support. Be reliable, trustworthy, and show appreciation for your friends.

Making New Friends

Engage in activities you enjoy, attend social events, and be open to meeting new people. Strike up conversations and show genuine interest.

Dealing with Conflict

Address conflicts calmly and openly. Communication is key in resolving misunderstandings and strengthening your friendship.

Quality over Quantity

Cultivate a few deep and meaningful friendships rather than focusing on a large number of superficial connections. Quality matters.
